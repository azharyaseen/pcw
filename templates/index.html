Frequently Asked Questions

Find the answers for the most frequently asked questions below

  • What is a price comparison website?

    A price comparison website is a platform that allows consumers to compare prices for a particular product or service across multiple retailers.

  • How do I use a price comparison website?

    To use a price comparison website, simply enter the product or service you are looking for into the search bar, and the website will display a list of retailers that sell that product or service, along with their prices. You can then compare the prices and choose the best deal.

  • Is using a price comparison website free?

    Yes, most price comparison websites are free for consumers to use. They make money by receiving a commission from the retailers for any purchases made through the website

  • How accurate are the prices on price comparison websites?

    Prices on price comparison websites are generally accurate, as they are updated in real-time. However, there may be occasional discrepancies due to delays in updating prices or technical issues.

  • Are all retailers included on price comparison websites?

    No, not all retailers are included on price comparison websites. Some retailers may choose not to participate, while others may only be included on certain websites.

  • Can I buy products directly from a price comparison website?

    No, price comparison websites do not sell products directly. They simply provide information on where to find the best prices for a particular product or service

  • Can I trust the reviews on price comparison websites?

    Reviews on price comparison websites are typically submitted by other consumers, and as such, their accuracy and reliability can vary. It's always a good idea to read multiple reviews and use your own judgment when making a purchasing decision

  • How do I know if I'm getting the best deal on a price comparison website?

    It's important to compare prices across multiple websites and retailers to ensure you are getting the best deal. It's also a good idea to check for any additional discounts or promo codes that may be available.

  • What types of products and services can I compare on price comparison websites?

    Price comparison websites can be used to compare prices for a wide range of products and services, including electronics, clothing, travel, insurance, and more.
